## About Me

I am a Research Scientist at [Google DeepMind](https://deepmind.google), working on Gemini for APAC since January 2026. I completed my Ph.D. at National Taiwan University in January 2025, advised by Professor [Yun-Nung (Vivian) Chen](https://www.csie.ntu.edu.tw/~yvchen/) in the [Miulab](https://www.csie.ntu.edu.tw/~miulab/).

My research focuses on reasoning and large language models (LLMs). For more details, please refer to my [resume]({{ site.cv_link }}).

## Experience

- **Google DeepMind** — Research Scientist, Gemini for APAC (2026–present)
- **Meta GenAI** — Research intern: enhanced reasoning with stepwise feedback and long-form reasoning
- **NVIDIA Research** — Research intern: error correction methods for multimodal language models
- **Amazon Alexa AI** — Research intern: factuality evaluation agent and synthetic data techniques
- **MediaTek Research**, **IBM Research** — Research internships
